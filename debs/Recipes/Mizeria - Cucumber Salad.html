Mizeria - Cucumber Salad

Source: Polish

Ingredients

1 large Cucumbers


salt


1/4 small onion ( or green onions) chopped finely


2 large radishes cut shoestrings


1 heaping TBSP (or 1/2 TBSP Creme Fraiche

1/4 tsp lemon


1/2 tsp honey


chopped fresh dill



Directions

First peel the cucumbers and slice them. Put them in a spinner and salt the cucumbers, then let them sit until some of the water gets drains from the cucumbers (10/15 mins should be okay). Add in onions and radishes then spin. Add dill.

Combine the sugar and lemon juice to the Cream Fraiche and set aside.

When ready to serve mix all and enjoy.
