Upma

Breakfast, Easy

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

1/2 tsp. Mustard Seeds

1 tablespoon Oil Or Butter

1 pinch Salt

2 tablespoons Peanuts (pistachios, cashews, or peanuts) chpped

1 teaspoon Cumin seeds

1 chopped Onion small

1 slices Chopped Ginger

1 tablespoon Channa Dal

1 pinch Hing

1 teaspoon Urad Wash

2 pieces Gr Chillies (1 jalapenjo)

8 leaves Curry Leaves



1 cup Sooji/semolina/rava

2 cups Water


1 tablespoons Lemon

1/4 tsp. Surgar


3 tablespoons Celontro chopped

Description

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t8_8Qg32TSg#t=264

Directions

Heat oil in a heavy pan cook and stir in one ingredient at a time: mustard seeds (let pop), stiringhanna dal & urad dal, peanuts, ginger, chillies, curry leaves, hing and salt. Cook until onions are transparent.

Add roasted semolina/sooji and stir, then add water slowly while stirring continuously to avoid lumps, mix well when all moisture is absorbed turn off heat and cover for 5 min.

Add sugar, lemon, and cilantro serve hot with butter or chutney on top.
