Lemon Pasta

Ingredients

3 Birds nest noodles or 2 cups fideo

1 cups chicken broth


1/2 stick butter

2 cloves garlic minced

1 small lemon rind & juice


1/4 cup cream or half-n-half

1 egg yolk

parm cheese shredded


Parsley chopped

salt to taste

Directions

Turn your Instant Pot to the saute function. When the display reads HOT add in the butter. Once the butter is melted add in the garlic and saute for 30 seconds. Add in lemon rind. Add in the chicken broth. Break the fettuccine noodles in half and lay them in the pot. The liquid should cover them almost completely.

Cover the pot and make sure the valve is set to “sealing.” Set the manual/pressure cook button to 1 minutes. When the timer beeps let slow vent for 5 minutes then vent the steam. Remove the lid and stir.

Add in the half and half, parmesan cheese & egg. Scramble the egg yolk and temper it with the hot liquid before adding it. Then add the lemon juice a little at a time, taste along the way, stopping when it tastes right to you. Add anything else you would like, personally I like to add asparagus that has been sautéed in a pan and cut up.

Salt and pepper to taste.

Top with fresh parsley
