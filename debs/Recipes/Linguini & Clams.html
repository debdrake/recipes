Linguini & Clams

Main Dish, Pasta, Seafood

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

1 16 oz package linguini pasta






1/2 cup butter

2 TBS olive oil

3 to 4 cloves garlic chopped

1/4 cup shallots

1/2 pound fresh mushrooms sliced



1/2 cup white wine

1/2 cup chicken stock

1/4 cup clam juice


~ 1 lbs chopped clams


1/2 cup fresh parsley chopped

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup parmesan grated

1/2 cup wine

Directions

Cook pasta and set aside.

Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Saute onions and mushrooms until golden brown, than add garlic and cook a minute longer.

Add wine and let it cook down.

Stir in clams with juice, parsley, salt and wine. Cook over medium heat until hot. Toss with pasta until evenly coated. Serve garnished with Parmesan cheese.
