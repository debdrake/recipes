Corn Bread

Breads, Side Dishes

Difficulty: Easy Source: Mary

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter softened

1/2 cup sugar

2 eggs

1/2 15 oz can creamed corn

1/2 4 oz can chili peppers drained & chopped

1/4 cup monterey jack cheese shredded

1/4 cup cheddar cheese shredded

1/2 cup flour

1/2 cup yellow corn meal

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease an iron skillet or 9x9 inch baking dish. In a large bowl, beat together butter and sugar. Beat in eggs one at a time. Blend in cream corn, chilies, Monterey Jack and Cheddar cheeses. Stir.

Add remaining ingredients; stir until smooth. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center of the pan comes out clean.
