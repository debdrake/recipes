Blueberry Ricotta Pancakes

Breakfast, Fruit

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

BLUEBERRY SAUCE

12 oz frozen blueberries or 2 cups

1/3 cup powdered sugar

1 lemon zest

1 tablespoons lemon juice or 1/2 large lemon

1 teaspoon corn starch + TBS cold water

1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract


PANCAKES

1 2/3 cups water

2 cups pancake/baking mix (I use Pioneer buttermilk baking mix)

1 cup ricotta cheese whole milk

oil

TOPPINGS

butter

whipped cream

Directions

Put the blueberries in a small saucepan with the sugar, lemon juice and zest. Bring to a simmer over medium heat and cook, stirring, until sugar dissolves and berries begin to burst. Mix cornstarch and water to make a slurry and slowly add to sauce. Let it thicken, then remove from heat and add vanilla. Strain blueberries and set aside the sauce for syrup.

Stir the 1 1/3 cup water with the pancake mix in a large bowl until moistened. Fold in the ricotta into the pancake mixture, then fold in the blueberries.


Heat a griddle over medium heat. Brush with the oil. Working in batches, spoon 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until golden brown, about 2 to 3 minutes per side. Melt a little butter onto of each pancake. Serve with syrup and whipped cream.
