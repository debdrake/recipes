Coconut Chutney

Sauces, Side Dishes

Difficulty: Easy Source: VahChef

Ingredients

1 cup Coconut fresh grated

3/4 cup Lite coconut milk

2-4 Green chilly sliced

1 small Baby Shallots (optional)

4 small pieces Ginger

1 handful Corriander leave optional

1/4 teaspoon Salt

1/2 teaspoon Sugar

Temparing

2 teaspoons Oil

1/4 tsp. Mustard seeds

1/2 tsp. Chana dal

1/2 tsp. Urad dal

Hing

1/4 tsp. Cumin seeds

2 Red Chili sliced (optional)

2-4 sprigs Curry leaves

Description

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PYGynTDEhhE

Directions

Take grated coconut, lite coconut, ginger, coriander, green chilly, sugar, salt, shallots, put all together in a blender and make a paste.

Heat oil in a pan add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal, chana dal, red chilly, cook this in the oil till lentils are golden brown, add hing, salt, curry leaves, pour the temparing on the top of the chutney and mix it well.
