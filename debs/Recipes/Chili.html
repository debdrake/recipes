Chili

Beef, Chili, Main Dish, Stews

Difficulty: Easy Source: De

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon cut & cooked

2 tablespoons greese from bacon

1 to 1 1/2 pounds chilli meat cooked in greese

2 15 oz stewed tomatos cans

1 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon brown sugar

1 stalk celery chopped

1 green pepper chopped

1 jalapeno pepper chopped

1/3 cup steak sauce

1/2 cup white wine

1/4 teaspoon cumin

1 can beef broth

Directions

Stir the first 5 ingredients in a medium bowl to blend. Stir in 2 tablespoons of the oil. Season mixture with salt and Mix all ingredients and bring to almost a boil, then cover, lower heat, and simmer for 3 to 4 hours. Best served over Mary's Corn Bread
