Crab Quiche

INGREDIENTS

  • Pitty Pat Savory Crust
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 cups cream
  • 8 oz. crab
  • 6-8 oz. cheese - swiss, guerere, grated
  • 1/2 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon zest
  • nutmeg, grated

METHOD

  1. 1. Make putty pat crust
  2. Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Layer in crust the cheese, crab, and onions. Whisk the eggs, cream, lemon zest, and pour into crust. Sprinkle with nutmeg and bake for 45-60 minutes, until a knife inserted into the middle comes out clean.
