Sourdough Pancakes

INGREDIENTS

  • 12 cups sourdough starter
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoons vegatable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanila

METHOD

Whisk eggs & milk, then fold into starter. Add oil & vanilla and mix. Sprinkle remaining spices and gently blend. Let batter sit for 15 minutes.
