Wall Wash

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. nutmeg
  • 24 ounces cream cheese, 3 - 8 ounce boxes
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/3 cup eggnog
  • 3/4 tsp. nutmeg
  • 2 small eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanila

METHOD

  1. 1. Melt butter, add to graham cracker crumbs, nutmeg and cinnamon. Form into pie pan, bake at 350 F for 10 minutes. Let cool.
  2. Cream cheese and sugar. Blend eggs, then eggnog, condensed milk, nutmeg, and vanilla. Pour in cheesecake mold, wrap in foil, then put in pan surrounded by warm water. Bake for 50-60 minutes at 350 F. Check by inserting a knife in the middle, and it comes out clean. SOURCE: A nod to Debsters Blueberry cheesecake recipe
