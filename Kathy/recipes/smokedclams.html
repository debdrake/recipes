Smoked clams in mushroom caps

INGREDIENTS

  • 24 mushroom caps, stemmed & cleaned
  • butter
  • 3-6 oz. cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 smoked clams, can, drained, reserve some liquid
  • garlic salt & pepper, to taste
  • parmesain or romano cheese, grated

METHOD

Arrange mushrooms on an oiled baking dish. Place a small square of butter in the bottom of each cap. Mix remaining ingredients, fill each cap. Top with parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 deg F for 20-30 minutes, until cheese is just browned.
