Best Corned Beef

INGREDIENTS

  • 2-4 lbs. Corned Beef Roast
  • 1/2 cup dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 5 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 & 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar

METHOD

  1. Boil corned meat for 2 hours in celery & onions.
  2. About 1/2 hour before meat is done, mix remaining ingredients in a small saucepan. Simmer for 5 minutes, spread on meat in baking pan. Bake at 350 deg F for 45 minutes.
