Pork Loin Chops with Green Onion Sauce

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 pork chops
  • 1/4 cup corn starch
  • Vegatable oil (peanut is best)

Marinade:

  • 1 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon wine (sweet rice wine is good, can be found in oriental food dept.)

Green Onion sauce:

  • 3 Tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 Tablespoon sugar
  • 4 Tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion (about 4)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon chili oil, or hot oil

METHOD

Combine marinade and put chops in it - marinade for 30 min. Combine green onion sauce and set aside. Heat oil in pan over medium heat. Dip chops in corn starch and brown in skillet for 2-4 min. each side. After all chops have been browned. Cover with green onion sauce and simmer for a couple more minutes. Serve over rice.
