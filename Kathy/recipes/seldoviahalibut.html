Seldovia Halibut Spread Sandwiches

INGREDIENTS

  • 1-2 fillets halibut
  • Mrs. Dash
  • 1 Tablespoon butter or margarine
  • mayo

METHOD

  1. Melt butter in frying pan. Liberally coat both sides of skinned fish with Mrs. Dash. Fry on both sides 4-6 minutes each side (depending on thickness). Cool, shred, and mix with mayonnaise; add more Mrs. Dash for taste.
  2. 1989, 1990 maybe? First time we had lunch provided on a halibut charter. It was a B&B charter operation, the husband ran the boat, the wife ran the B&B and made the lunches. The sandwiches were this spread in the middle of a small roll. Simple, but delish! Use as a sandwich spread or on crackers. SOURCE: Seldovia Fishing Adventures
