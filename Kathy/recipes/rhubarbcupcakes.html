Rhubarb Cupcakes (or Cake)

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups rhubarb, chopped fine
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Cream Cheese Frosting
  • 1/4 tsp lemon zest

METHOD

  1. Mix all ingredients, rhubarb through cinnamon, and pour into cupcake paper using a 2 inch scoop, filling the cups about 1/2 way*. Bake at 350 def F for aprox. 20 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  2. *I want a generous amount of frosting that comes just to the top of the cupcake paper. It travels better.
  3. Make Cream Cheese Frosting, then add 1/2 tsp lemon zest. Frost the cooled cupcakes. SOURCE: Elaine Larson, Taste & See Cookbook
