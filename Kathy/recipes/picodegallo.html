Pico De Gallo

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 head cabbage, sliced thin & cut into bite-size
  • 6-8 green onions, chopped coarse
  • 2-3 red tomatoes, seeded & diced (roma are best)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup (or 2 whole limes) lime juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves, coarsely chopped.
  • salt & pepper, to taste

METHOD

Mix garlic with lime juice, add all other ingredients. Serve with tortilla chips.
