Peanut Butter Cookies - The Best

INGREDIENTS

  • 304 grams peanut butter, crunchy
  • 308 grams shortning
  • 400 grams brown sugar
  • 5 Tbsp milk
  • 2 small eggs
  • 2 tsp vanila
  • 240 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Peanut Butter Frosting

METHOD

  1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Mix brown sugar, peanut butter, shortening, eggs, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Add dry ingredients, and mix. Use a 1 1/2 inch scoop, about 1.2 Tbsp, to drop cookies on the sheet. This dough is wet, so you donâ€™t have to press down as for regular pb cookies. Just drop and cook for 7-8 minutes until just browning at the edges.
    Cool.
  2. Make Peanut Butter Frosting. Use the frosting to make sandwiches out of two cookies.
