Olive Tapenade

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 jar pimento stuffed green olives
  • 1 can black olives
  • 1 large jalepeno pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • olive oil

METHOD

Mix all ingredients. Brush on or wipe on, wipe down with hot water. Cuts through grease & dirt. No streaking! SOURCE: Jan Stenga

  1. finely dice olives, jalepeno, and garlic - pulse on a quisinart works well, I start with the jalepeno, it seems to need more dicing. I also cut out the seeds and spine, I don't like it too spicy.
  2. drizzle with olive oil, mixing until you get a spreadable consistency.
  3. serve with french bread or crackers
