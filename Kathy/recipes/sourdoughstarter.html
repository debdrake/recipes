Sourdough Starter

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup Sourdough Starter
  • 2 & 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 cups warm water (warm enough it is almost hot to the touch)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 tsp yeast, if needed

METHOD

Mix and let sit overnight for at least 8 hours. Save 1 cup in the starter jar, use the rest for bread, pancakes, etc.
