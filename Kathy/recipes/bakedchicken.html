Baked Chicken

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 apple, cored and quartered
  • 1 onion peeled and quartered
  • 3-4 stalks celery, cleaned & cut in 2-3 in. lengths
  • Salt, parsley flakes
  • butter

METHOD

  1. 1. Clean out chicken, then dry with paper towels. Liberally sprinkle inside with salt and parsley. Put in baking pan, and stuff both openings with apples, onions, and celery. Lightly salt & pepper outside of chicken. Bake in 350 deg. oven for 1 hour, or 325 deg. oven for 1 Â½ hours. Every 15 minutes, take chicken out and rub a stick of butter all over outside of chicken. Serve with stuffing, mashed potatoes and gravy.
  2. Gravy: Drain out all but 2 Tablespoons of grease in baking dish, and add 2 tablespoons of flour. Mix thoroughly with grease and cook until golden brown. Add 2 Cups of chicken broth, stirring constantly. Best to add 1 cup of broth, stir with flour mix, then add the last cup gradually to get desired thickness -- you may not need all 2 cups.
