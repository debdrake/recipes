The Best Biscuits

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 4 tablespoons cold butter, cut into tiny cubes
  • 1 1/2 cups cold buttermilk

METHOD

You need a big cake pan with about 1/2 cup of flour spread out to form the biscuits, a 1/4 cup measure generously sprayed inside and out with cooking spray, and a 9 in ch round pan (greased and floured) to cook them in. Preheat oven to 500 degrees. Pulse dry ingredients in a food processor, then scatter butter and pulse until it looks like cornmeal. Transfer to bowl, add buttermilk and stir until just mixed. Dough will be very wet. Use the 1/4 cup measure to scoop level amounts of dough, and drop in big floury cake pan. You should have enough for 12 lumps. With floured hands, gently pick up each lump and form into ball. Shake excess flour and place in round pan. 9 balls go around the perimeter, and 3 in the middle. Drizzle melted butter on the top of each ball, then bake for 5 minutes. Turn heat down to 450 degrees, and finish baking until biscuits are a deep golden brown, about 15 more minutes.
