Scallop Salad

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon hot english mustard
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons scallions or chives, chopped fine
  • 8 small red skinned potatoes

METHOD

  1. 1. Boil potatoes in salted water until tender, around 12 minutes. Cool completely & cut in 1/8" slices.
  2. Make dressing: whisk 1 T oil, vinegar, mustards. Then whisk mayo & cream. After desired consistency, season with salt & pepper to taste.
  3. Heat oil, slide in potatoes and saut√© until golden brown. Remove & join with scallops.
