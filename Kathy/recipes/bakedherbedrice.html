Baked Herbed Rice

INGREDIENTS

  • - 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup onion, minced
  • 2 cups rice , uncooked
  • 3 cups broth or water
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, dried
  • 1 teaspoon thyme, dried
  • 1 bay leaf
  • tabasco, to taste

METHOD

Preheat oven to 400 deg F. In a dutch oven or heavy pot, saut√© onion in butter. Add cleaned rice and cook until golden brown. Add remaining ingredients and bring to a boil. Cover and bake for 17-20 minutes.
