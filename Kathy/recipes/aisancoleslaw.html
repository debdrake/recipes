Aisan Cole Slaw

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 head of cabbage, shredded
  • 1 bunch of green onions
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 1 cup almonds whole, toasted
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 2 pkgs of ramen soup noodles, broken up (dried)
  • Dressing:
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 pkgs of seasoning from ramen soup packet
  • dash of soy sauce and sesame oil

METHOD

Combine dressing ingredients. Toast almonds, sesame seeds, and soup noodles in 350 deg. oven for about 8-10 minutes. Mix salad just before serving then add dressing and serve. This does not keep well! Serve & eat immediately.
