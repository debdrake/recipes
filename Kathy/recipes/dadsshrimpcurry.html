Dad's Shrimp Curry

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb. shrimp, raw, peeled & deveined
  • 1 & 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 Tbsp. butter
  • 2 Tbsp. flour
  • 1/2 onion (optional), sliced thin
  • 1/2 pepper - orange/yellow/red (optional), sliced thin
  • 1 Tbsp. curry powder
  • salt & pepper

METHOD

  1. 1. Start rice cooking about an hour before dinner is to be ready. 30 minutes later, melt butter, saute onions and peppers for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Stir in flour and cook until flour starts to change to a golden color. Add salt, pepper and curry. Stir. Add broth and cook (stirring often) until it starts to thicken.
  3. Add the shrimp, cook for 1-2 minutes, then turn and cook for another 1-2 minutes. Serve with condiments: sliced green onions, diced bananas, pineapple, raisins, nuts, coconut flakes, mango chutney.
