Spinach Bacon Egg Salad

INGREDIENTS

  • spinach, fresh, cleaned
  • mushroom, cleaned, sliced
  • red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 per person egg, hard boiled
  • 4-6 bacon, chopped, cooked
  • 1 tsp. grey poupon mustard
  • 1-2 Tbsp. shallot, finely chopped
  • 1-2 Tbsp. cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp. sugar

METHOD

Put spinach, mushroom, and red onion in salad bowl. Cook bacon until crisp, then remove from bacon fat - you should have about 2 Tbsp of fat - skim off excess if necessary. Saute shallots until soft, then add rest of ingredients and cook for a couple of minutes. Salt and pepper to taste. Pour over spinach and toss. Top with chopped egg and bacon.
