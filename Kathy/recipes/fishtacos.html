Wall Wash

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups pico de gallo
  • 1 lb. white fish fillets, cod & rockfish best, halibut pretty good.
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
  • Mexican rub - my favorite is a lime & chili mix
  • Corn tortillas
  • Grated cheddar and pepper jack cheese
  • Chopped Avacados
  • Sour cream

METHOD

Rinse and pat dry skinned, boned (as well as possible) fillets. Rub with olive oil, season with salt & pepper, and generously rub with chili mix. Blacken on both sides in a well oiled iron skillet. Place on warm plate, set in 300 degree oven. Warm up tortillas in cleaned and re-oiled skillet, serve with refried beans, cheese and pico de gallo.
