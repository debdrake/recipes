Blackened Rockfish Salad

INGREDIENTS

  • - 1-2 Rock fish fillet, skinned
  • 12 0z salad greens
  • 1 Tbsp. Keith's Salmon Rub
  • 1/2 cup Maple Vinagarette
  • shaved Parmesan
  • croutons

METHOD

  1. Mix dressing, prepare salad greens
  2. Coat fish with olive oil and salmon rub. Blacken for 3-4 minutes per side on high heat.
  3. Toss salad in dressing, add parmesan and croutons and top with fish.
