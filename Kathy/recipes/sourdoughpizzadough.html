Sourdough Pizza Dough

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 & 1/2 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 cup milk, warmed
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 & 1/2 to 3 & 1/2 cup flour

METHOD

  1. Add the milk, salt, sugar, and oil to the starter and blend well. Add the flour slowly, stirring constantly. Knead on a floured surface. Put the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a towel, letting it rise until doubled.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 deg F. Divide in two, roll out for pizza crust. Top & bake for 15-25 minutes.
