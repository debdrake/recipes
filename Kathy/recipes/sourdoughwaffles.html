Sourdough Waffles

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups sourdough sponge
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla

METHOD

  1. Mix ingredients, let sit for 15 min.
  2. Heat up waffle iron to med-high temperature (350-375 F) for 3 minutes on each side. Add 1 cup of batter, close, cook 3 minutes on one side, flip, 3-4 minutes on the other side.
