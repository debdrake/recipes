Bobby's Crispy Cookies

INGREDIENTS

  • - 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups flour, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1 cup rice crispies
  • 1/2 cup nuts, chopped
  • 1 cup cranberries - dried, optional

METHOD

Preheat a 350 deg F oven. Cream butter & sugars, add wet ingredients, then dry. Drop on cookie sheet, press with a fork and bake for 12-14 minutes.
