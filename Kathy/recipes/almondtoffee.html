Almond Toffee

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup almonds, toasted
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1-2 cups chocolate chips
  • 2 cups walnuts, finely chopped

METHOD

  1. 1. Toast almonds in the oven, spread on a cookie sheet. Mix butter, sugar, vanilla and salt in a pot, bring to a boil, stirring constantly until 270-280 deg F.
  2. Pour toffee mil over almonds, and sprinkle chocolate chips on top. Let chips sit for a couple of minutes, then spread evenly over toffee. Sprinkle with walnuts, an let cool. Break into pieces.
