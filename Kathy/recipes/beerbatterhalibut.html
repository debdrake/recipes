Beer Batter Halibut

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. halibut, chunked/li>
  • 1 cup flour/li>
  • 1/4 cup corn starch/li>
  • 1 Tablespoon Lowry's seasoned salt/li>
  • 12 oz beer/li>
  • Tartar Sauce<

METHOD

  1. Mix dry ingredients (use the 1/8 c corn starch), then add beer. Don't add the whole can, it will make batter too thin. Add about 2/3 of the can, then mix and check for thickness. It will thicken as it stands for a few minutes, but you want the consistency of pancake batter.
  2. Skin and wash halibut, dry in paper towels, then chunk in 2 inch squares. Dip fish in 1/2 c corn starch, then batter, and fry in 1‚Äù deep hot oil until golden brown. Flip once, takes aprox 3 minutes.
