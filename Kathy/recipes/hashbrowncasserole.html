Wall Wash

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 lbs. Frozen Hash Browns
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped fine
  • 16 ounces sour cream
  • 2 cans cream of celery soup
Topping:
  • 2 cups corn flakes or potatoe chips
  • 1 sticks butter

METHOD

Preheat oven to 350 deg F. Mix filling ingredients. Mix butter and corn flakes/chips, and top casserole. Bake for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
