Crab Dip

Appitizers

Difficulty: Easy Source: De

Ingredients

1 8 oz packages cream cheese room temperature

1/2 lbs, lump crabmeat flaked

1/2 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup parmesan cheese grated

3 tablespoons grean onions minced

2 cloves garlic minced

2 teaspoons worcestershire sauce

2 teaspoons lemon juice

1 tablespoon horseradish sauce

cheese for topping

Directions

Bake for 35 to 40 minutes until lightly golden on top.
