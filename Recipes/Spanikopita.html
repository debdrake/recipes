Spanikopita

Holiday, Side Dishes, Thanksgiving, Vegetables

Difficulty: Easy Source:

Ingredients

1/2 package bacon cut up

1 large onions chopped

2 cloves garlic minced

2 4 oz feta cheese packages

2 large bags spinach cooked and drained

2 tablespoons fresh dill

2 tablespoons flour

1/2 cup parmesan grated

4 eggs lightly beaten

salt & pepper to taste

1 package phyllo dough

1 stick butter melted

1/2 cup pine nuts roasted or toasted

Directions

Cook bacon and remove all but 1TBS of grease. Add onion and cook about 1 minute, add garlic and stir for 1 more minute. Remove from heat and let cool. then add in feta cheese, eggs, salt, pepper, bacon, flour, and spinach.

Layer phyllo dough flat in deep pi shell or square baking pan and brush with butter. Place a small amount of spinach mixture onto each piece of dough, layer with roasted pine nuts, and top with parmesan cheese. Keep layering and top with phyllo dough and brush with butter.

Cover with foil and bake at 350 in preheated oven 25 minutes, uncover and let bake 20 minutes longer until golden brown.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Brush a little butter into each cup of a 12-cup muffin tin.

Lay out a stack of 3 sheets Phyllo dough (keeping the remaining 3 sheets moist - see notes.) Cut stack into 4-inch squares, so you have 6, 3-layered squares. Gently brush the squares with the remaining butter and press into each of the muffin cups. Repeat with the remaining 3 sheets.

Divide the spinach mixture evenly among the muffins cups; fold the overhanging phyllo dough into the center.

Bake for 15 minutes until golden and set.
