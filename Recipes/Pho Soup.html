Pho Soup

Instant Pot, Low Carb, Soups

Difficulty: Easy Source: YouTube

Ingredients

BROTH

1 large Shallot

1 ounce Ginger

Oil coat the pan

2 pounds Beef soup bones

1 tablespoon Rock sugar

1/2 Cinnamon stick

2 Star Anise

2 Cloves

1/2 teaspoon Corriander dried and ground

1 teaspoon Salt

1 1/2 tablespoon Fish sauce

TOPPINGS

Steak (cheap) seared in pan and sliced

Basil chopped

Mint chopped

Celontro chopped

Bok choy chopped and seared in pan

Noodles cooked in hot water and drained

Bean sprouts

Hoi Sin Sauce
