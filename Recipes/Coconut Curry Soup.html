Coconut Curry Soup

Soups

Difficulty: Easy Source: Eat, Live, Run

Ingredients

1 tablespoon cocunut oil

1/2 onion choppeed

2 cloves garlic minced

1/2 jalapeno pepper chopped

2 tablespoons ginger grated

10 curry leaves fresh

1 6-inch stalk lemongrass

2 teaspoons thai curry paste

4 cups chicken broth (1 box)

1 can coconut milk

1 lbs. raw shrimp or chicken deveined, tails on or off

1 cup mushrooms diced

2 tablespoons sugar

1 tablespoon fish sauce

2 tablespoon lime juiced

chives or green onions chopped for garnish

= celontro chopped for garnish

mint chopped for garnish

Directions

In a medium pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, chili, ginger, curry, and lemongrass, and red curry paste and cook, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes, or until onions are softened.


Add chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce head and simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.


Strain, then add in coconut cream or milk, chicken breast (or tofu or shrimp), and mushrooms. Simmer until chicken breast pieces are just cooked through, then add fish sauce, coconut, and lime juice, plus more of each to taste.


Cook 2 minutes, then ladle into serving bowls and top with sliced green onions and fresh cilantro and mint.
