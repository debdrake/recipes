Beef Stroganoff

Beef, Dinner, Main Dish

Difficulty: Easy Source: De

Ingredients

1 1/2 lbs. beef cut into 1/2" slices

1 cup allegro marinade

1 tablespoon olive oil

1/2 onion chopped finely

2 tablespoons butter

3/4 lbs, mushrooms sliced

(null) salt & pepper to taste

nutmeg grated

1/2 teaspoon basil dried or 1 TBS fresh

1/4 cup white wine

1 cup sour cream

noodels (egg or linguini)

Directions

Marinate beef a least an hour in allegro

Cook pasta per directed

Brown meat in oil (throw away remaining marinade). Add onion, and cook a couple more minutes. Remove from pan, and set aside.

Saute mushrooms in melted butter. Add beef back in and add the rest of the ingredients. Serve over noodles.
