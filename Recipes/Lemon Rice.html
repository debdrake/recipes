Lemon Rice

Rices, Side Dishes

Difficulty: Easy Source: VahChef

Ingredients

1 cup Boiled Rice

2 tablespoon Oil

3 Red Chili

1/2 tsp. Mustard seeds

1 tsp. Chana dal

1 tsp. Black gram

10 grams Cashew or peanuts

1/2 tsp. Fresh ginger chopped

2 sprigs Curry leaves

1 pinch Hing

6 Green chili sliced

1/4 tsp. Turmeric powder

Salt to taste

1 tsp. Cumin seeds

1/2 tsp. Sugar

1 tablespoons Lemon juice + zest
