Skillet Pizza

Easy, Main Dish, Pizza

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

DOUGH

1 1/4 cups water warm

1 packages yeast or ~100 grams starter

1 tablespoon honey

1 tablespoons olive oil +

2 teaspoons salt


3 to 3.5 cups flour



TOPPINGS

cheese to taste

onions (optional) sliced " "

fruit (like pears or tomatoes) sliced " "

nuts chopped " "

fresh greens (like arugula, spinach, or basil) " "

1 ounce meat

Directions

Combine the water, starter, honey, 1 TBS olive oil, and 1 cup of flour mix on medium-low speed or by hand. Cover and leave on counter for at least 12 hours before adding the rest.

Add 2 to 2.5 cups of flour and salt to make the dough.

Shape into 3 or 4 for medium balls, put in tall containers and refrigerate. When you're ready to use - set it out for 2 to 3 hours, so it comes to room temperature and rises a bit.

Shape pizza dough and spread dough thinly in large skillet. Add cheese, etc. My favorite is Mozzarella cheese with pears, prosciutto, walnuts, and arugula, and maybe a bit of parmesan if i have it. Note: sauce is not necessary - it's up to you.

425 for 20 minutes unless you're adding greens at the end. If using greens cook 16 minutes, add greens (if like arugula or spinach) and cook for 4 more minutes.
